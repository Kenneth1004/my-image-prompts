# Cyberpunk Chongqing — Scene Prompts

## Character Reference (consistent across all scenes)

Female cybernetic operative, full-body cyborg special forces agent. Athletic lean East Asian woman with soft natural features, warm dark brown eyes, beauty mark below left eye, natural freckles scattered across her nose bridge and cheeks, natural muted peachy nude lips, matte realistic skin texture. Short choppy textured messy pixie cut of near-black dark brown hair, thick side-swept bangs partially covering left eye. Wearing a full-body form-fitting hybrid tactical exosuit in pearlescent off-white / pale bone white finish — segmented composite hard/soft suit with exposed metal panel lines, rivets, articulated plating, perforated mesh panels at joints. High tight collar, intricate mechanical corset-style articulated support structure running down spine. Both forearms fitted with heavy fully cybernetic modular gauntlets with exposed hydraulics, wiring, utility attachments. Integrated white high-block-heel ankle boots with small ankle buckles. Stoic calm neutral professional expression, quiet unshakable competence.
